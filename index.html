About Me

Hi, I’m Grant, full name Douglas Grant Heath.

I'm a Computer Programming student at Durham College, where I’ve learned about full stack development, databases, mainframes, PC hardware, web development, and more.
While at Durham College I gained experience with C#, Python, Java, JavaScript, and more. What captured my interest most in software development was the ability to bring ideas to life and interact with them.

This, paired with my love for games, made it clear that I wanted to focus on game development. Since my education didn’t cover game dev deeply, I began teaching myself Unity in early 2025.

Using resources like YouTube, Stack Overflow, GitHub, and Unity documentation, I’ve created multiple projects that helped me learn by doing — making mistakes, problem-solving, and improving with each step.

My programming fundamentals from school made the transition smoother, but I know there’s always more to learn and improve on. Game development is a continuous learning journey that I’m excited to keep pursuing. I hope this portfolio shows both where I started and where I’m heading in my game development journey.
