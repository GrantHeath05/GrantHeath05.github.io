Junkyard Tycoon Project

Start Date: October 25th, 2025 - Finish Date: in Development



Game Idea

The idea for this game is the player lives in a junk yard where they can explore and collect junk. Junk can be sold or reused to get money or new items.
The more money and success the user has the more money they can invest into the junkyard to continue to grow the business.



Player Mechanics

  • Walking - the player can move freely in all directions since the game uses a top-down, bird’s-eye camera view using WASD or Arrows.
  • Inventory System - will be able to store multiple small items in inventory or 1 medium item in hands.
  • Large Item Handling - current plan is to add a forklift for transporting 1 large item at a time

Core Mechanics

  • Sales - player will be able to quick sell items for a fixed price
  • Auctions - Auction system will allow the player to potentially sell items for a larger amount but it will take longer and has a chance to sell for less
  • Scrap for Parts - Crafting system to unlock new parts of the game, can craft things like a forklift


People Involved in Project

For this project I will personally be doing everything from scratch on my own. I am not an artist so im learning as I go.



Character Customization

One of my main goals for this project is to challenge myself and try new things, which is why I decided to make the player fully customizable. This includes different skin tones, clothing, hats, hairstyles, and facial features.

Since I’m not an artist and creating a player sprite from scratch is already a challenge, I approached it in a modular way. I first created a blank white base sprite for the player, then in Unity I will tint each individual piece of the player. For other layers such as the shirt, pants, hair, and face, I made separate objects so that each part can be tinted and customized individually.

Im not too sure how most games do it, but this approach is the easiest way I could think of to accomplish a fully customizable character.
