Junkyard Tycoon Project

Start Date: October 25th, 2025 - Finish Date: N/A



Game Idea

The idea for this game is the player lives in a junk yard where they can explore and collect junk. Junk can be sold or reused to get money or new items.
The more money and success the user has the more money they can invest into the junkyard to continue to grow the business.

Core Mechanics

  • Sales - player will be able to quick sell items for a fixed price
  • Auctions - Auction system will allow the player to potentially sell items for a larger amount but it will take longer and has a chance to sell for less
  • Scrap for Parts - Crafting system to unlock new parts of the game, can craft things like a forklift
